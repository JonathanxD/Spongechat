Para ver e poder acessar o Help:
- spongechat.help
- spongechat.admin
- spongechat.mod

Para criação e remoção de Canais:
- spongechat.command.channel.(create|remove|delete)
- spongechat.command.channel.*
- spongechat.(createchannels|removechannels|deletechannels)
- spongechat.admin

Controle de Mute de Canais:
- spongechat.command.channel.(mute|unmute)
- spongechat.command.channel.*
- spongechat.(mutechannels|unmutechannels)
- spongechat.admin

Controle de Kickar Players de Canais:
- spongechat.command.channel.(kick|expulse)
- spongechat.command.channel.*
- spongechat.kickplayers
- spongechat.(admin|mod)

Controle de Mute de Players:
- spongechat.command.player.(mute|unmute)
- spongechat.command.player.*
- spongechat.(muteplayers|unmuteplayers)
- spongechat.(admin|mod)

Controle de Ban de Players em Canais:
- spongechat.command.player.(ban|unban)
- spongechat.command.player.*
- spongechat.(banplayers|unbanplayers)
- spongechat.admin

Controle de CIM (Click In Message) para abrir Book de informações:
- spongechat.cim.info
- spongechat.cim.*
- spongechat.(admin|mod)
